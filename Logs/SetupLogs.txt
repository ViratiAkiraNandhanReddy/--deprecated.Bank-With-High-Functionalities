Setup Logs
  * Purpose: To record the progress and completion of the setup process.
  * File Name: SetupLogs.txt
  * Content:
      * Timestamp of each setup step.
      * Details of the step (e.g., terms accepted, product key validated).
      * Status of the setup process.
  * Example:
        [INFO]:[Setup.py][15-May-2025 @ 10:20:00 AM] - Terms and conditions accepted.
        [INFO]:[Setup.py][15-May-2025 @ 10:25:00 AM] - Product key validated successfully.
        [INFO]:[Setup.py][15-May-2025 @ 10:55:00 AM] - Setup completed successfully.
