Roadmap

Our plans for the future of Bank-With-High-Functionalities include:

  • Mobile App Integration:

    Develop Android and iOS versions for better accessibility and convenience.

  • AI-Based Fraud Detection:

    Implement AI models to detect and prevent fraudulent transactions in real time.

  • Multi-Language Support:

    Add support for multiple languages to cater to a diverse user base around the globe.

  • Dark Mode:

    Introduce a sleek dark theme for improved accessibility and user comfort.

Development Timeline

  • Q1 2025: Launch of the mobile app for Android and iOS.
  • Q2 2025: Integration of AI-based fraud detection.
  • Q3 2025: Rollout of multi-language support.
  • Q4 2025: Introduction of dark mode and other accessibility features.
