Usage Guide

Learn how to use the application effectively to make the most out of its features:

  1. Creating an Account:

    Navigate to the "Create Account" section and fill in the required details, such as your name, email, and password.

  2. Performing Transactions:

    Log in to your account, go to the "Transactions" section, and choose from Deposit, Withdraw, or Transfer options.

  3. Loan Management:

    Apply for loans in the "Loans" section, track their status, and manage repayments seamlessly.

  4. Viewing Transaction History:

    Access the "History" section to view and filter your transaction logs based on date, type, or amount.
